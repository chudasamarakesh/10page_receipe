DAY 5 CELEBRATION

NAVRATRI

Navratri, the nine-night festival dedicated to the worship of the Hindu goddess Durga, is a vibrant and spiritually enriching celebration observed by millions of people across India and the world. Each day of Navratri is dedicated to one of the nine forms of the goddess, and on the fourth day, devotees worship Goddess Kushmanda. This essay explores the significance and rituals associated with Navratri Day 5.
