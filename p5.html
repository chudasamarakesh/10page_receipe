garba night

People dress in colorful and traditional clothing, with women wearing vibrant chaniya cholis and men opting for kurta-pajamas. This adds to the festive atmosphere.i

Navratri encourages social bonding as friends and family members participate in the celebrations, exchange gifts, and share meals..

Early in the morning, devotees gather for prayers and recite mantras dedicated to Goddess Brahmacharini. These prayers seek her blessings for purity, knowledge, and spiritual enlightenment
